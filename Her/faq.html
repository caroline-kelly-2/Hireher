FAQ

Why do we need the FDI?

We need Female Designers of Ireland because the ratio of women to men currrently working in the design idustry is 25:75, despite the fact that 63% of students studying design are female. This indicates a fundemental problem within our industry here in Ireland. Where are the women?

Not only are women outnumbered across the industry, in higher managerial roles, only 17% are occupied by women. While we don't have the answers to why exactly this is, we know that awareness of these statistics will help change them.

Why should the industry be more diverse?

Besides the fact that each person should receive equal oppurtunities, and should not be discriminated against based on gender, race sexual orientation etc., many studies show that the more diverse a business is, the more successful and profitable it is. See 'Why diversity matters’, 'Women On Boards-global Trends In Gender Diversity On Corporate Boards', 'Firms with More Women in the C-Suite Are More Profitable'

What can I do?

Start the conversation!
The more attention we bring to the inequalities, the more likely it is to change.

Pledge to mentor a younger female designer

Join FDI if you identify as female.
We can send you newsletters with information about the design scene here in Ireland, and you get access to our events before anyone else. You also get to use the FDI logo on your website and promotional material.

Attend an Event hosted by FDI At this event you can you can meet other women in the design industry. As well as doing some networking, you can keep up to date with UI solutions or learn a new coding language at one of our workshops.

Join the online directory
On the FDI website you can submit a personal profile of yourself or your studio and be included in our online directory.

If you are in a hiring position or a managerial role:
Considering putting a ‘back to work’ plan in place for those on maternity leave.

Use the flexi-time system of work. This allows a more flexible work day for each of your employees while ensuring a productive work space.

I'm a man, can I join?

Yes! The goal of FDI is to be as inclusive as possible and give a voice to all. We encourage men (and people of all genders), to be part of the conversation. We welcome everyone at our talks, events and workshops. However, as we are first and foremost a women focused group, we are including only those identifying as female in our online directory.

I’m not based in Ireland, can I join?

We are, for now at least, focusing on female designers within the island of Ireland. But we are aware of equality needed across the global design industry. We reccommend looking at other international organisations promoting women creatives:
The Creative Lady Network
Women who Draw: An Open Directory of Female* Illustrators
Women Who Design
The Womens Center for Creative Work
Women Who
Invisible: Women in Australian Graphic Design
