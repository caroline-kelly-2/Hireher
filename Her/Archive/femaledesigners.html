Women in Design

We strive to incubate a love for design within young females, and inspire them to know that they themselves can not only work within the design community, but take on leadership positions such as creative directors and managers


75:25 endevers to create a space which allows for participation and engagement, from men and women, on the issues of inequality and diversity in design. 75:25 is a source of promotion, encouragement, and learning for female designers. Based in Dublin, Ireland, we have an emphasis on the Irish design industry and it's lack of diversity. The name, 75:25, stems from the ratio of men to women working in the Irish design industry. A startling figure by itself, but when its accompanied by other statistics such as 'only 17% of design managers and 14% of designers at foreman or supervisor level are female.'¹


75:25 aims to educate people on these inequalities, while reinforcing the talent which exists within the female design community.
