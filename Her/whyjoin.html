Why Join?


We can send you newsletters with information about the design scene here in Ireland

Get access to our events before anyone else

Get to use the FDI logo on your website and promotional material

Attend an Event hosted by FDI
At this event you can you can meet other women in the design industry. As well as doing some networking, you can keep up to date with UI solutions or learn a new coding language at one of our workshops.

Join the online directory
On the FDI website you can submit a personal profile of yourself or your studio and be included in our online directory.
